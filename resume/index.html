About Me

Hello! My name is Sarah and I am a graphic designer and illustrator based in Orlando. I have always been interested in art and creating things that are beautiful. I went back to school to learn graphic design so that I could combine my love of art with my desire to create useful designs for people. I am currently studying at Seminole State College, and hope to learn as much about digital media as possibe. I am a mom to 4 children and more than anything I love to create art with them. There is something magical about watching art come to life for them. In my spare time, I enjoy photography, silver-smithing, and spending time with my family.
